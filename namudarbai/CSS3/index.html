Best places to visit in Sicily

Taormina

Even if it is a small town on the east coast of Sicily, Taormina is one of the most visited touristic resorts in all of Italy. Perched up on a hill about 250 m above the sea, Taormina is about midway between Messina and Catania, a good base for day excursions in eastern Sicily.

Cefalù

Loved by tourists and locals, this beautiful Sicilian corner is the ideal location for relaxing sunbathing and charming sunsets. Cefalù is a sea destination in the Province of Palermo, located on the northern coast of Sicily.
